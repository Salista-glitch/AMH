About Us

Building a Future Together Through Every Child, Classroom and Community

African Montessori Hub is a transformative, Afrocentric think tank committed to reimagining and advancing Montessori education across the African continent. Rooted in the principles of liberation, equity, and holistic development, we believe that education should not only nurture the individual child but also empower communities.

Our mission is to align the timeless values of Montessori with the rich cultural wisdom of Africa—creating environments where children are not only learners but also thinkers, creators, and agents of change. We strive to centre African identity, languages, and traditions in the learning journey, ensuring that every child grows with a sense of pride, purpose, and belonging.

We envision a future where every child in Africa has access to quality, child-centered education that encourages independence, compassion, and social responsibility. Because when we honour the child, we honour the future of Africa.
